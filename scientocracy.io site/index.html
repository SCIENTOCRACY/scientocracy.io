About

Scientocracy is free speech decentralized science project made in the Ethereum blockchain for rational people performing and discussing science.


The Scientocracy project:

  • is an incentivized and decentralized blockchain-based social platform with a focus on science related content.
  • has its own ecosystem composed by its own political, economical and social systems to ensure a dynamic approach.
  • is built for individuals inmersed in the STEAM fields (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art and Math).

Scientocracy's goals are to:

  • awaken the scientists in all of us
  • become a leading brand that provides decentralized science
  • connect the experts and geniuses of today with those of tomorrow
  • create next-gen tools for individuals in high levels of science
